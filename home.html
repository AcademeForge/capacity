Getting started

Three steps and you're in. Name your circle, add at least one person, then set your first state. That middle step — the invite gate — is intentional: Capacity is literally useless alone, so onboarding forces the moment that creates a second user. You can search by @username or share an invite link. No phone number required, ever.

03

The app itself

Four screens cover everything. Your home shows your circle color-coded by their current state — you see at a glance that Ravi needs space, Sara is doing well, Jamie is okay. Tap your own card to update your state in one tap. Tap a person to message them, with their live state always visible in the chat header so you know whether to reach out or hold off.
